About LeBron James

Born and raised in Ohio. He is a 39 year old African American basketball player in the NBA. He went first pick of the first round in the 2003 draft class. Coming straight out of highschool to the professional level at only 18 years old.He was drafted to the Cleveland Cavaliers. Which led him to receiving a endorsment deal from Nike of almost 90 million dollars. He has invested in several schools and own a school of his own. He has donated to charity events and have helped communites over the country. It's safe to say he makes plays on and off the court.

Career Highlights

LeBron James first career championship was in 2012 against the Thunder. He has made it to the championship 10 times in his career which is more than any other player in history. He has only won 4 out of 10 of those visits and is still seeking to get another one hopefully with his son Bronny by his side. He has scored the most points in a career and in playoffs, he also made the All-NBA teams 19 times.He is the only player to ever score 40,000 points and it is very hard to take the greates of all time title from him.
